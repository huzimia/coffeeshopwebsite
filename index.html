Meet Our Experts

Liam Petersen

Pastry Chef

Liam Petersen is the pastry chef responsible for creating the delectable baked goods offered at the coffee shop. With a background in culinary arts, he brings creativity and precision to the kitchen, making sure every pastry complements the coffee perfectly.

David Smith

Lead Chef

David Smith is the shift supervisor who ensures the coffee shop runs smoothly during his shifts. He coordinates staff, manages inventory, and handles any customer issues with grace.

Aiden van der Merwe

Barista

Aiden van der Merwe is a skilled barista who excels in crafting a variety of espresso-based drinks. He has an eye for detail and a passion for perfecting latte art. His friendly demeanour and extensive knowledge of coffee make him a favourite among regular customers.
